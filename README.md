##  Overview
This repository contains a *generic Information Security Policy* designed to help organizations protect their digital assets, ensure regulatory compliance, and promote a culture of security awareness.

This policy was originally developed during my internship, aligned with industry best practices and standards such as **ISO/IEC 27001** and **SAMA Cybersecurity Framework**.  
This version is sanitized and contains no confidential company-specific details.

---

## Purpose
The purpose of this Information Security Policy is to:
- Define the principles, roles, and responsibilities for information security.
- Protect sensitive data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
- Comply with relevant legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements.
- Promote a secure environment for staff, contractors, and third parties.

---

##  Policy Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third parties.
- All information systems, networks, and applications.
- All forms of information: electronic, printed, verbal, and visual.
